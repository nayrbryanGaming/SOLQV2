# Privacy Policy for SOLQ

**Effective Date: April 17, 2026**

SOLQ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information in our non-custodial payment orchestrator application.

## 1. Non-Custodial Architecture
SOLQ is designed as a non-custodial interface. We **never** have access to, store, or transmit your private keys, seed phrases, or sensitive wallet credentials. All transaction signing occurs locally within your chosen third-party wallet provider (e.g., Phantom, Solflare).

## 2. Information Collection
- **Wallet Addresses**: We may temporarily process your public wallet address to facilitate on-chain verification and transaction history.
- **Transaction Metadata**: We collect generic transaction details (amount, timestamp, merchant NMID) to provide payment status and orchestration services.
- **Diagnostics**: We may collect anonymous crash reports and performance telemetry to improve service reliability.

## 3. Data Usage
We use collected data solely to:
- Orchestrate QRIS payments via Solana liquidity partners.
- Provide real-time transaction status updates.
- Comply with mandatory financial reporting requirements where applicable.

## 4. Third-Party Services
SOLQ integrates with third-party providers including:
- **Solana Blockchain**: Publicly viewable ledger of all authorized transactions.
- **Jupiter Aggregator**: For decentralized asset conversion.
- **Settlement Partners**: Licensed financial institutions handling fiat disbursement.

## 5. Your Rights
As a non-custodial user, you maintain full control over your assets. You may clear your local application cache at any time via the app settings.

## 6. Contact
For inquiries regarding data safety, contact: support@solq.id
